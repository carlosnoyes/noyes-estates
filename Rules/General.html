House Rules & Expectations

These rules exist to keep the home clean, respectful, and comfortable for everyone.
By living here, all tenants are expected to follow them.

1 Guests

No overnight guests without written permission.

No more than one guest at a time without approval.

2 Cleanliness & Shared Spaces

Clean up after yourself immediately; leave shared spaces as you found them.

Maintaining shared spaces may require periodic extra cleaning by tenants.

3 Noise, Courtesy & Conduct

Noise should be kept to reasonable levels at all times. Quiet hours are from 11pm to 9am.

Courtesy and respectful language toward housemates and management is required.

4 Smoking & Substances

No smoking of any kind inside the house. Smoking outside only; dispose of cigarette butts properly.

No open flames of any kind (candles, incense, etc.).

5 Pets

No pets or animals without written permission.

6 Utilities & Energy Use

Conserve utilities (turn off lights, close windows during heating/cooling).

Mini-fridges and space heaters must be approved before use.

7 Keys & Security

You are responsible for your room key.

$50 fee for lost or replacement keys.

8 Personal Belongings

Keep personal items in your room unless approved.

Landlord is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

9 Landlord's Access

Management and contractors may access both public and private areas.

If you see anything suspicious, please contact management.

Thank You for Your Cooperation

We appreciate your respect for these house rules ♥
