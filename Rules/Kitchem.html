Shared Kitchen Guidelines

Renters share responsibility to keep the kitchen clean.

Wash, dry, and put away your dishes every time you use them.
Wipe down counters, stove, and sink every time you cook.
If the trash can lid cannot close completely, take out the trash and leave an empty trash bag behind you.
Store your food and personal items only on your marked spaces.
Wash, dry, and fold dirty kitchen towels so there are always clean ones ready.

Let's keep this space clean and usable for everyone!
